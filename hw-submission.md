## Homework Submission Steps


1. First, open your GitHub App. Make sure that the drowndown in the upper left of the window has the `Current Repository` for the repo you want to submit.

	![](https://i.imgur.com/osNfjKX.png)

1. Down at the bottom left of the window, put a message in the field next to your profile image: `<YOUR-NAME>: Submit <NAME-OF-HOMEWORK>`

1. Next, click `Commit to master`. 

	![](https://i.imgur.com/3yeYSx1.png)

1. Then, click on the `Push origin` button to push your code up to `YOUR` GitHub account.

1. Go to the repo you just pushed up on GitHub.com. The one on your profile.

	![](https://i.imgur.com/zf8NoNl.png)
	
1. Click on the "New pull request" button.

	![](https://i.imgur.com/jIJeNOO.png)

1. Click on "Create new pull request".

	![](https://i.imgur.com/HZW0o3O.png)

1. Make sure that you put your full name in the title box for the pull request. Then click "Create pull request".

	![](https://i.imgur.com/xXZoH5t.png)
	
1. That's all! You're done!

	![](https://i.imgur.com/CkaHZHx.png)
